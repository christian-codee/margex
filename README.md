## Where Do "Scam" Allegations Come From?

Before we get into Margex specifically, it's worth understanding how "scam" labels spread in the crypto space.

The pattern is almost always the same:
- A trader loses money on a leveraged position and blames the platform
- A withdrawal gets delayed due to KYC verification and the user panics
- A competitor or bad actor posts negative reviews to damage reputation
- Forum threads and Reddit posts get copy-pasted across the web, compounding over time

None of these automatically make a platform fraudulent. Losses from leverage trading are part of the risk. Verification delays are regulatory compliance. And online negativity is something every exchange from Binance to Kraken deals with constantly.

So the real question is: are there *structural* red flags with Margex? Things like missing funds, fake volume, exit scams, or withheld withdrawals without justification?
